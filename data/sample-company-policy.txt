TechGadgets Inc. - Employee Handbook

=== Remote Work Policy ===
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week, subject to manager approval.
Remote work arrangements must be documented and reviewed quarterly.
Core hours for remote workers: 10 AM - 3 PM (your local timezone).
All remote workers must be available on Slack during core hours.

=== Vacation Policy ===
Full-time employees receive:
- 0-2 years: 15 days paid vacation per year
- 3-5 years: 20 days paid vacation per year
- 6+ years: 25 days paid vacation per year

Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.
Maximum consecutive vacation days: 10 days (without special approval).

=== Health Insurance ===
The company covers 80% of health insurance premiums for employees.
Coverage includes medical, dental, and vision.
Dependents can be added at employee's expense.
Open enrollment period: November 1-30 each year.

=== Professional Development ===
Annual budget for professional development: $1,500 per employee.
Can be used for:
- Online courses and certifications
- Conference attendance
- Books and learning materials
- Professional memberships

Requires manager approval before purchase.

=== Code of Conduct ===
All employees must:
- Treat colleagues with respect
- Maintain confidentiality of company information
- Report conflicts of interest
- Follow security protocols for data handling
- Attend mandatory annual ethics training
